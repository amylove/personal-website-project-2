About

Hey there, fellow tech explorer! I'm Amy, a passionate user experience designer with a knack for turning digital chaos into seamless experiences. My journey in tech started with a curious mind and a love for problem-solving—kind of like teaching a cat to use a touch screen (challenging yet rewarding!).

When I'm not crafting intuitive interfaces or untangling UX puzzles, you'll probably find me surrounded by a pack of dogs and cats, each with their own opinions on my latest design drafts. They're my unofficial UX testers, keeping me grounded and reminding me that simplicity is key, even when dealing with the most discerning users (looking at you, feline friends!). So, let's create digital magic together—because every great website deserves a tail-wagging response!
